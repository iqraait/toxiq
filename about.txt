<div class="conference-description">
    <p>
        <strong>ToxIQ 2026</strong> is a premier national conference in Clinical Toxicology,
        bringing together Clinical Toxicologists, Emergency Medicine Physicians,
        Critical Care and Intensive Care Physicians, Internal Medicine Physicians,
        Pediatricians and Pediatric Intensivists, Forensic Medicine Specialists, Clinical pharmacists and other Health care professionals
        on a common platform to share expertise, discuss current challenges, and
        advance the science and practice of Clinical Toxicology and Poison Management.
    </p>

    <p>
        The conference is designed to provide practical, evidence-based updates
        through expert lectures, interactive panel discussions, debates, clinical
        case-based learning, scientific presentations, and networking opportunities.
        Participants will gain insights from renowned national faculty and engage
        in meaningful discussions on contemporary issues and emerging trends in toxicology.
    </p>

    <p>
        In addition, <strong>ToxIQ 2026</strong> will feature a range of academic
        competitions aimed at encouraging research and scholarly engagement,
        including the <strong>Postgraduate (PG) Quiz Competition, Oral Paper
        Presentations, and Poster Presentations</strong>. These sessions provide
        an excellent platform for students, trainees, and young researchers to
        showcase their knowledge, present their research findings, and interact
        with experts in the field of toxicology.
    </p>
</div>
